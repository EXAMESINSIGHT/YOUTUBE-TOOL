YouTube Transcript Tool

Get transcripts for any YouTube video—captioned or not—with this simple, free method!

Step 1: Copy the YouTube Video URL

Find the video you want to transcribe and copy its URL from the address bar. Example: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dQw4w9WgXcQ

Step 2: Check YouTube’s Built-in Transcript

Open the video, click the three dots (…) below the player, and select "Show transcript." If available, copy the text. If not, proceed to Step 3.

Step 3: Download the Audio (For Videos Without Transcripts)

Use a free tool to extract the audio:

  • Online Option: Go to ytmp3.cc, paste the URL, select "MP3," and download.
  • Advanced Option: Install yt-dlp and run: yt-dlp -x --audio-format mp3 "YOUR_URL" -o "audio.mp3"

Step 4: Transcribe the Audio

Upload the MP3 to a free transcription service:

  • Otter.ai: Free up to 600 minutes/month—upload and get your transcript in minutes.
  • Flixier: Free browser-based option—upload or paste URL if supported.

Copy the transcript to your clipboard or save it as a file!

Note: This method works for any public YouTube video, even without captions. For personal use only—respect copyright laws!
